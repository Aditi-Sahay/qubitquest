🌀 What’s a Qubit?

A classical bit is 0 or 1. A **qubit** can be 0, 1... or both at the same time. Like a spinning coin mid-air!

🔗 Quantum Entanglement

Entangled qubits are mysteriously linked. Flip one, and the other instantly reacts — even light-years away.
